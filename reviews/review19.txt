The donkatsu ramen and watermelon boba smoothie were both really good.The ramen broth was rich with umami flavor  with lots of noodles, vegetables and nice meaty slice of pork.

The price for the ramen was a little higher than I would have expected, but I overall I was pleased.

Vegetarian options: The menu showed a vegetarian option

Parking: Akita is in the Cache Valley mall food court which has plenty of parking

Wheelchair accessibility: The mall has handicap parking and automatic doors to get into the mall.
